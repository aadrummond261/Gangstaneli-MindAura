🌌 Gangstaneli MindAura
🚀 Elevator Pitch
Gangstaneli MindAura is a mental wellness support web app that helps users understand their emotional state through text and emoji check-ins while providing simple, real-time support and guidance. It is designed for individuals managing stress, anxiety, or mood changes who want a more reflective and helpful daily check-in experience.
🧠 Problem Statement
Many mental health apps focus only on tracking moods, but they do not help users understand or respond to how they feel in the moment. Users often check in without receiving meaningful insight or support, which limits the usefulness of these tools.
There is a need for a simple platform that not only captures emotional input but also helps users reflect, recognize patterns, and take small actions to improve their mental state throughout the day.
👤 Target User
A college student or young adult who experiences daily stress, anxiety, or emotional ups and downs and wants a quick, simple way to check in with themselves and receive helpful feedback.
This user may already be managing their mental health and wants a tool that feels supportive, easy to use, and not overwhelming.
💡 Why This Project
I want to build Gangstaneli MindAura because mental health is something many people deal with daily, including stress and emotional ups and downs. I am interested in creating something that not only tracks how someone feels, but actually helps them understand and respond to it.
This project allows me to combine my interest in technology with something meaningful that could support people in their everyday lives.
